The Story

Where it began

Poemetric began as a personal love for words and expression and grew into a space for poetry and storytelling. It has evolved into a collaborative anthology featuring writers from different parts of the world.

The name is a blend of poetry and metric — measuring the depth of human experience through verse. Every piece is chosen for its honesty, craft, and the way it illuminates something we all recognise.

"Words carry the weight of what cannot be spoken —
measure them carefully." — Poemetric
